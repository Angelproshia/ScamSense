How Detection Works
  • Transaction amount is the primary feature used by the model
  • Server-side time of submission is used — late night transactions carry higher risk
  • Large round-number amounts are flagged as behavioral anomalies
  • Transaction type influences the model's risk profile
  • A Random Forest classifier outputs the final fraud probability
